## About

I am a Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Wisconsin–Madison. I am a member of the [Wisconsin Privacy and Security Group](https://wiscprivacy.com/), where I am fortunate to be advised by [Prof. Kassem Fawaz](https://kassemfawaz.com/). I also collaborate closely with researchers from the [MadS&P Group](https://madsp.cs.wisc.edu/). My research broadly focuses on trustworthy machine learning, with particular interests in fairness, and auditing of machine learning systems. I also work on developing robust machine learning systems, including LLM-based agents, for security and privacy applications. Currently, I am focusing on designing web agents and evaluating them on user-centric security and privacy tasks.
  
My research journey began at [IIT Madras\` Speech Lab](https://asr.iitm.ac.in/) (now called SPRING Lab) where I worked on developing multi-lingual ASR models for India languages in a low-resource setting, under the guidance of [Prof. Umesh Srinvisan](https://www.ee.iitm.ac.in/umeshs/) on an Indian-Government funded Speech to Speech Translation Project. Since then, I have had the opportunity to build speech recognition models in industry through internships at Amazon Alexa Speech, where I worked on representation learning for ASR with [Dr. Gopinath Chennupati](https://cgnath.gitlab.io/) in 2022, and at Otter.ai, where I worked on contextual deep biasing for ASR with [Kritika Singh](https://scholar.google.com/citations?hl=en&user=Ltk3SykAAAAJ&view_op=list_works&sortby=pubdate) in 2025.

Previously, I received my B.Tech. in Electronics and Communication Engineering from the National Institute of Technology Tiruchirappalli in 2020 and my M.S. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of Wisconsin–Madison in 2023.

Outside of research, I enjoy watching Football (or Soccer, as it is called in the United States) and Cricket. I play tennis and go for runs around the scenic Lake Mendota in an ongoing effort to burn the excess calories I gain from eating tasty Indian food. At one point in time, I took interest in performing and writing music; these days, I just stick to listening and appreciating progressive musical forms, from Indian classical to progressive metal. Also, one day I hope to hike via-ferrata in [Murren, Switzerland](https://muerren.swiss/assets/images/4/Klettersteig%20M%C3%BCrren_1-byb87bbhzc942zh.webp) and watch a Champions League fixture at [Stamford Bridge](https://img.chelseafc.com/image/upload/f_auto,c_fill,g_faces,w_1440,h_856,q_90/editorial/news/2026/01/22/champions-league-view-stamford-bridge-fireworks.jpg) supporting my team Chelsea FC.

Feel free to reach out if you'd like to discuss research, or any of my other interests.
